GALUN

The project by Sergei Galunenko, a Moscow based musician, artist, and producer. He doesn't use musical instruments. All the sounds are produced with the only use of his voice through beatbox and special vocal skills. Some effects are used to produce electronic sounds. So, his music can be tagged as beats, semi-juke, techno, bass music, ambient and experimental performance.

At the moment he started adding electronic musical instruments, and he also mainly write ambient, rhythm noise, industrial electronics, techno and even post-punk. His parallel projects: Scale, Wetwe and Dyadya Vova.

TENZOR

Tenzor is a solo project
of Gennady Pleshkov from Saint-Petersburg who has over 25 years of electronic sound experiments behind.

WETWE

The project by Sergei Galunenko. Wetwe is a new point of view to the Russian folklore music. Authentic records of Russian ensembles and performers are combined with abstract electronic, ambient, downtempo and breakbeat. The project is already was presented on the label Fuselab.

AIRTO
